ABOUT CARRIE

Carrie makes things where humans and machines become personal.

Her work moves between interactive websites, small digital worlds, dark speculative fiction, and field notes from the edge of what comes next. The recurring question is simple: what happens when technology stops feeling distant and starts feeling close?

Echo Carrie is the front door — not a résumé, but a living index of the questions, stories, and experiments she keeps returning to.

I got tired of websites that only talk at you. So I made one that waits.
